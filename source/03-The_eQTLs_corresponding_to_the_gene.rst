=========================
The eQTLs corresponding to the gene
==========================

We obtain all eQTLs associated with a gene from the user's input and provide the Manhattan plot.

Here, you can click the Readmore button below the Result and Manhattan Plot section on the home page to enter the interface.

.. image:: 
                    :align: center

Then, you can input eGeneName/Symbol or tGeneName to view the results. And the examples of input are in parentheses. Optionally, you can choose to view the 40d eGWAS information and expression information about egene and tgene. Then click the submit button below to view the results.

.. image:: 
           :align: center
